About

We set out to build an intuitive site that collects data about universities, cities, and majors from several sources and presents it to you, a prospective college student, in a way that makes it easier for you to find the information you need to pick the right college. The site has information about all of the universities in the U.S., including key statistics about their admissions and costs for attending. It also has essential information about the most common majors that college students can choose from. Finally, the site contains snapshots of college cities and factors that may influence a prospective studentâ€™s decisions about what college to attend.

By aggregating data about universities, majors, and cities, we have centralized much of the key information that students need to choose the best college for them. While university databases and sites that list majors provide helpful information, our site provides information specifically relevant to prospective and current college students.
